About Me

Thanks for checking out my website! Here you'll find my experience, qualifications, interests, and aspirations.

I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a BS in Industrial Engineering. I took a variety of computer science and statistics courses after I decided to pivot into a career in data science.

After graduating, I returned to Istanbul and worked as a Junior Data Scientist for two years at TurkNet, one of the largest internet providers in Turkey. I will be attending the University of San Francisco for an MS in Data Science this summer.

As someone of mixed heritage, I am also a big advocate of cultural identity. Despite not growing up in the United States, I related heavily to the Asian American experience. I've been blessed with many positives and experienced hardships because of my Turkish-Taiwanese background. While not directly related to data science or the tech industry, I try to actively keep these virtues in mind when faced with ethical dilemmas or scenarios.
